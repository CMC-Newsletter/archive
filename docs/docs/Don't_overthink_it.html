Gone in a flash ⚡
The public sale of 59.8 billion BitTorrent Tokens (BTT) on the Binance launchpad, worth roughly $7.2 million, sold out in minutes. There was a total of 966 participants. 😲

The Binance launchpad, a token sale platform, held the sale in 2 sessions - one for those paying with Tron (TRX) tokens, and the other for those paying with Binance Coin (BNB). Each BTT token was valued at $0.00012 for the sale, and had a minimum purchase amount of 100,000 BTT.

According to Tron founder Justin Sun, the BTT tokens sold out within 14 minutes. Binance founder Changpeng Zhao then mentioned that sale could have ended in mere seconds if not for technical issues with the launchpad platform due to the "astronomical" demand. 🤯
Justice league 🛡️
4 crypto exchanges in South Korea, Bithumb, Coinone, Korbit, and Upbit have partnered on an initiative to combat potential money laundering and customer protection. 🤝

They will create a hotline to share real-time wallet information on suspicious crypto trades, and seek to identify trades with suspected links to phishing, predatory lending, pyramid schemes and other illegal activities. The exchanges will also operate a shared database of suspicious wallet addresses to help them identify and stop scammers from using different exchanges to move large quantities of cryptocurrencies to the same wallet. 🙌

The 4 exchanges are also encouraging other crypto exchanges to join the initiative.
Straight to the source 👌🏻
Multicoin Capital has led a $2.5 million seed round in The Graph, a company that is building an open source software that indexes data from public blockchains, such that anyone building decentralized applications can quickly access them. 💰

According to Multicoin Capital, companies that use these data often times build their own data tools to extract the relevant data. What The Graph is doing is proposing to index everything in a way everyone can use forever, so no one will need to build a tool to do so. 🙏🏻

Other investors that contributed in the seed round include Compound VC, CoinFund, DTC, Kilowatt, Reciprocal Ventures, SPC and others.
