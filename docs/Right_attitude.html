Broadened scope 👀
Tether is looking to launch its stablecoin (USDT) as a native token on the Tron blockchain. It has partnered with Tron to launch the dollar-pegged USDT stablecoin as a TRC-20 token. Currently, Tether only exists as a stablecoin built on the Bitcoin and Ethereum blockchains

The addition of this TRC-20 USDT will allow users to more easily transact with decentralized applications (dapps) and protocols based on the Tron blockchain, and thus improve decentralized exchange liquidity and improve Tron's accessibility to institutional investors. 👌🏻

The new version of USDT is expected to launch in Q2.
Making amends 🔨
Following the online criticism over its acquisition of the blockchain analytics firm Neutrino, Coinbase will be letting go of several employees. 👋

According to Coinbase’s CEO, Brian Armstrong, the firm will be letting go of Neutrino’s employees who has worked for the IT startup called Hacking Team, regardless of whether they are not currently affiliated with the startup. In the past, Neutrino’s top management has led projects for Hacking Team, a startup who aided governments known for human rights abuses. 🤦‍♂️

According to Armstrong, Coinbase "had a gap in our diligence process" in that while they "looked hard at the technology and security of the Neutrino product, we [they] did not properly evaluate everything from the perspective of our [its] mission and values as a crypto company.” 🧐
Gotta stay afloat 🏊‍♀️
According to a report from The Information, crypto finance startup Circle Internet Financial is looking to raise a further $250 million in funding via a combination of equity and debt financing. 🤑

Circle offers several services, including crypto buying via its Circle Invest app and an over-the-counter (OTC) trading desk for institutional clients.

The firm previously raised at least $246 million and was also backed by Goldman Sachs, but now seeks more funding due to the prolonged cryptocurrency bear market (according to the report). 🐻

In other news, Circle has also just closed its acquisition of the equity crowdfunding platform SeedInvest, so as to pave the way for the company to tokenize securities in the future.
Blockchain term of the day 🤓 - Gas price
A term used on the Ethereum platform that refers to the price you are willing to pay for a transaction. Setting a higher gas price will incentivize miners to prioritize that transaction over others.
