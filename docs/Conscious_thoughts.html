Retest 👊
Over the weekend, total market capitalization experienced a significant run up from ~$135.3 billion to ~$141 billion throughout Friday, and stayed at that level for the remainder of the weekend. 📈

Also over the weekend, BTC found its way back above the $4,000 mark! It is currently trading at ~$4,020, and is currently challenging the 100-day EMA line on the daily chart, a level that it failed to break through during the early to mid Feb run up last month. Similarly, on the weekly chart, BTC is challenging its 50-day EMA line, a level it failed to break through several times in late Nov to early Jan. Hopefully, this time... 🙏🏻

Change of plans ◀️
Crypto exchange Bittrex recently cancelled an “initial exchange offering” (IEO) for the RAID project just hours before it was set to launch. 🛑

RAID (XRD) had planned to raise $6 million, but Bittrex called it off after discovering that its partnership with e-gaming data analytics company OP.GG had been terminated. According to Bittrex, that partnership “was a vital part of the RAID project” and thus "did not feel that it was in the best interest of its (Bittrex's) customers to move forward with the IEO.” 
Feed me 🤑
A blockchain project focused on interoperability, known as Tendermint, has recently raised $9 million in a Series A funding round led by venture capital firm Paradigm. Other participants include the likes of Bain Capital and 1confirmation, among others. 💰

This funding injection will go toward Tendermints’s continued development of the Cosmos Network, through the expansion of its team. Tendermint has also recently launched Cosmos Hub, the blockchain designed to improve interoperability between any number of other blockchains, after close to 3 years of planning. 
Business as usual 👌
According to a spokesperson, CME Group has made 'no changes' to its Bitcoin Futures plans. 🗣️

This reassurance is notable in light of the news that its rival derivatives exchange operator, CBOE, recently announced that it would not add a Bitcoin futures contract in March this year, meaning that once the last currently traded futures expires in June, this market will be halted until new futures get listed.

At this point in time, CME's Bitcoin Futures volumes has been approximately double that of CBOE's. 😲
Blockchain term of the day 🤓 - Genesis block
The first block of data that is processed and validated to form a new blockchain, often referred to as block 0 or block 1.
