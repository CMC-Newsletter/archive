Inked the deal ✍️
6 international banks have signed letters of intent with IBM, to issue stablecoins on the IBM payment network "World Wire", powered by the Stellar (XLM) public blockchain. 🤝

At the moment, 3 of the 6 banks have been identified, such as RCBC (Philippines), Banco Bradesco (Brazil), and Bank Busan (South Korea).

World Wire will supposedly let regulated institutions move funds across borders more efficiently and cheaper than through traditional banking processes. It has payment locations in 72 countries, and supports 48 currencies. 😲
It's a ruthless game 😔
South Korean crypto exchange Bithumb plans to cut up to 50% of its staff and reduce its employee count to about 150. 👋

According to Bithumb official, many of the employees will be leaving on their own volition as “voluntary retirement is part of our [its] support program for former employees and is intended to provide assistance and training for job placement”. Additionally, Bithumb's "trading volume has decreased compared to the previous year, [so] we are trying to provide internal measures." 😔
