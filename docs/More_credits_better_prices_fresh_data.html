We’ve heard your collective feedback over the last two months, and are happy to announce  many enhancements to the CoinMarketCap API which should make it all the more functional for you.

• As we merge our Public API into the Professional API on December 4, we've doubled down on our commitment to maintain a free version of our cryptocurrency API. Our free plan is now called Basic and gives you 66% more call credits per month.

• We've introduced a new Hobbyist plan at $33 for personal use, with a significant 4x upgrade to 40K call credits from the free plan, and access to our dedicated price conversion endpoint; perfect for you to start running larger experiments for your projects.

• For our commercial customers, our updated introductory $79 Startup plan (formerly Hobbyist) comes with a whopping 100% increase in call credits. The $299 Standard plan gets a 66% boost in monthly credits as well.

• Delivering on a popular request, Startup or higher plans now have access to latest OHLCV numbers, at any point in the day. Instead of waiting until the current UTC day completes, you can now get the latest OHLCV stats every 5 minutes!

• All plans may now fetch the latest crypto rankings more often: Our latest market rankings endpoint now returns up to 200 coins per API credit! This allows even our Standard tier to fetch ALL currencies every minute with credit to spare.

• Also by popular demand we've released a dedicated testing sandbox for the Professional API! You may go to sandbox.coinmarketcap.com to trial any of our subscription plans for free using a snapshot of our market data to determine your needs. When ready, switch to a production API Key and you’re ready to go!

This is the first in a series of regular updates we'll be rolling out with our Professional API service. Stay tuned for some additional enhancements coming soon like historical cryptocurrency and exchange market ranking endpoints,  exchange level historical market pair data, and hourly OHLCV.
