Risky business
Switzerland's Financial Market Supervisory Authority (FINMA) has issued a confidential letter to the Swiss Expert Association for Audit, Tax and Fiduciary (EXPERTsuisse), which revealed its stance on how financial institutions should weight crypto assets in their loss-absorbing capital buffer calculations.

The letter revealed that FINMA advises financial institutions to assign a "flat risk weight of 800% to cover market and credit risks" against crypto assets. For example, if the current price of BTC is $6,000, institutions would have to value each coin on their books at $48,000 (8x) when deciding on an adequate buffer.
