Fib retest 👊
Over the last 24 hours, total market capitalization rose again from ~$176.8 billion to ~$179.8 billion, with most coins seeing gains in the ~1-5% range. 24 hour volume registered ~$43 billion, pretty much at the average level of the week. BTC is currently trading at ~$5,290, up ~1% over yesterday. On-ramp 🌉
According to crypto exchange Binance's CFO during Paris Blockchain Week, Wei Zhou, the exchange will launch its new fiat-to-crypto platform in Singapore next week. 😀

According to him, the new Singapore buy/ sell on-ramp will be a "very easy buy/sell platform so users in Singapore can buy and sell Bitcoin (BTC) with Singapore dollars.” For now, users will only be able to purchase BTC, but the exchange hopes to provide more crypto options in the future, adding that "for a lot of these regulated jurisdictions, it’s easy to start with things that people know and understand and you can gradually build on that.”
Pervasion ✊
Crypto exchange Coinbase has expanded its crypto-to-crypto trading services to 11 more countries. 😲

Customers in Argentina, Mexico, Peru, Colombia, Chile, India, Hong Kong, South Korea, Indonesia, the Philippines and New Zealand can now access its services. They are able to access services on both Coinbase.com as well as Coinbase Pro (Coinbase's professional trading platform). 😊

Coinbase now has a presence in 53 countries, spanned across 4 continents. It's crypto-to-crypto trading activity has also overtaken its fiat-to-crypto trading activity, and now represents 51% of its overall trading volume figures (caa Feb 2019). 
Rising sun coming through 🤘
Japenese e-commerce and internet company Rakuten, has started accepting account registrations for its new cryptocurrency exchange, Rakuten Wallet. Currently, only customers with an account at Rakuten Bank or with an existing Rakuten member ID are allowed to sign up. 

Rakuten also plans to launch a mobile app allowing users to trade cryptocurrencies, and to make deposits and withdrawals. 📱

Rakuten Wallet is licensed by the Japanese Financial Service Agency and is registered with the Kanto Local Financial Bureau as a virtual currency exchange service provider under the country’s Payment Service Act.
Blockchain term of the day 🤓 - Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD)
A trend-following technical analysis momentum indicator that shows the relationship between two price moving averages. The calculation is done by subtracting the 26-day exponential moving average (EMA) from the 12-day EMA.
