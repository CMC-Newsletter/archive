Wheels in motion 🎡
The NYSE Arca has filed the paperwork for a Bitwise Bitcoin ETF proposal. 📝

The proposal was filed on 10 Jan 2019 and elaborates on the methodology of the Bitwise Bitcoin ETF which is best known for its intended use of a regulated third-party custodian to store the Bitcoins (BTC). The proposal notably elaborates that prices for the ETF will be "“weighted such that Bitcoin prices from exchanges with a greater amount of the trading volume in the prior hour are weighted more heavily than Bitcoin prices from exchanges with lesser amounts of volume.” 🤔

While NYSE Arca has filed the proposal, the clock has not yet started for its approval or rejection due to the ongoing U.S. government shutdown - the SEC’s deadline for deciding an ETF proposal is set upon its publication in the Federal Register.
Signs of strength 💪
Crypto startup best known for their pursuit in advancing the idea of security tokens, Polymath, has locked up 75 million of its POLY tokens (7.5% of its total supply) for 5 years. 👀

According to Polymath cofounder Chris Housser, this move which comes at a time which many refer to as the crypto winter, demonstrates that its "treasury is healthy" and that they "don’t have a need for these tokens at this time”. He also mentioned that 74% of the locked-up tokens will come from company funds and 26% will come from founders' tokens. 

Housser also mentioned that the current strongest immediate interest for security tokens is in real estate, as “people really like the idea of fractionalized ownership and the ability to own a piece of something that would be much more difficult to do in the traditional world." 😊
Punt 💰
Institutional digital assets manager Morgan Creek Digital has invested $3.1 million in a seed round for tokenized real-estate startup, RealBlocks. 💰 The funding round was led by investment firm Science Inc., and also included others such as Zelkova Ventures, Ulu Ventures and Cross Culture Ventures.

According to the founder of RealBlocks, Perrin Quarshie, the investment will go towards the development of its Ethereum-based real estate platform. The platform will enable firms to raise capital through tokenized securities, investors to directly purchase real estate using both cryptos and fiat currency, and provide investors with access to offerings from private equity funds worldwide using fiat or cryptocurrencies. 
Blockchain term of the day 🤓 - HODL

A type of passive investment strategy where you hold an investment for a long period of time, regardless of any changes in the price or markets. The term first became famous due to a typo made in a bitcoin forum, and the term is now commonly expanded to stand for “Hold On for Dear Life”.
